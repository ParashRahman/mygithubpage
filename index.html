I recently completed my Master's degree at University of Alberta under Richard Sutton and Joseph Modayil. My thesis (linked here) was dedicated to advancing continual learning agents, agents that receive one experience at a time and must learn to act in their environment with limited feedback.

I am interested in uncovering the principles of intelligence that allows for the manufacturing of intelligent agents. The intelligent agent should behave similarly to biological agents with simple, computationally fast, and memory efficient algorithms.

For fun and inspiration, I enjoy philosophy, psychoanalysis, psychology, and politics. (Updated: March 23, 2021)
